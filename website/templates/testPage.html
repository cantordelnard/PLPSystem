PREDICTING TIMELY PROGRAM COMPLETION OF BSIT STUDENTS USING LOGISTIC REGRESSION ANALYSIS

A logistic regression analysis was used to predict timely graduation rates for Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) students at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasig (PLP) based on their academic, socioeconomic, and behavioral factors. The model comprises a dataset ranging from student records from the classes of 2020 to 2023 and has an accuracy of 86.23% in predicting students at risk. Interventions are carried out on the identified factors of at-risk students. Students with academic challenges will have additional academic requirements to support their learning. Those under the socioeconomic factor will be encouraged to participate in events to equip them with relevant resources and support for their circumstances. Students with behavioral challenges will be encouraged to consult the guidance counselor on their concerns. These interventions are prompted when the system identifies a student at risk of not graduating on time. These also contribute toward increasing graduation rates at PLP, which leads to the effective use of institutional resources and better student outcomes.
